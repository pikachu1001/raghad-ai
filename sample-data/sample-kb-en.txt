# Raghad AI — Sample Knowledge Base (for testing)

## Consultation Services

Raghad AI provides business consultation services for startups in Saudi Arabia. Services include commercial registration guidance, local compliance support, and operational planning.

## Dialect-Aware Support

Users can ask questions in Saudi dialect such as local Arabic expressions. The system maps dialect terms to formal knowledge base content for accurate retrieval.
